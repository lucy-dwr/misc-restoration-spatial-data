# EBMUD 2026-05-22-v01 Decisions

Decision log for the East Bay Municipal Utility District submission standardized
by `scripts/clean-ebmud-2026-05-22-v01.R`.

## Decisions

- Submitted `project_stage` value `Complete` is standardized as
  `post-construction monitoring and science`.
- Submitted yes/no `funding_secured` values are represented as dollar values:
  `Yes` is standardized as `estimated_budget - funding_gap`, and `No` is
  standardized as `0`.
- Submitted combined funding-source string
  `DWR FAIR Funding Agreement & EBMUD Capital` is split into two
  semicolon-delimited funding sources:
  `DWR FAIR Funding Agreement; EBMUD Capital`.
